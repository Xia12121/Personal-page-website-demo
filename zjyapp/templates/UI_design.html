Graduates go to statistical website UI design

Beijing Normal University - Hong Kong Baptist University Joint International College Graduate Employment Statistics Website (GPS)

In this project, my teammates and I jointly designed and built the statistics website of graduates' destination based on Django architecture, and my main work was responsible for the UI design of the website.

Introduction of Functions

The website is a forum website, users can register, log in, comment, post blog, like, pay attention to and share their employment/study destination. This website aims to serve UIC students according to their own conditions such as GPA, IELTS scores, target colleges and regions to screen the admission cases of seniors and seniors, and help users complete their career planning.

My task mainly focused on the UI design. In this part, we optimized the user's operation process and improved the quality of business services by simulating user operations for many times.
