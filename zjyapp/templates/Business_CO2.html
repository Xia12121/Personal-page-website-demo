Carbon Economy Service Platform Business Plan

The "Simplistic Carbon" APP project is created by an interdisciplinary team from BNU-HKBU United International College (UIC), focusing on providing carbon economy related services to individuals and enterprises. The project aims at practicing the operation model of "Internet + Carbon Economy", and has developed a carbon footprint calculation and rating system independently.

Introduction of my Work

As the conceptualizer and co-founder of the Carbon Simple project, I was responsible for the project's publicity, marketing, basic business logic design and public relations. The project won second prize in the National College Students' E-commerce "Innovation, Creativity and Entrepreneurship" Challenge and has received support and recognition from experts and investors in the carbon economy. We have also established a strategic partnership with Guangdong Huien Environmental Protection Technology Co. I look forward to the opportunity to enter the Master of Digital Media (MDM) program in the future and contribute to the further promotion of carbon footprint metrics visualization.
